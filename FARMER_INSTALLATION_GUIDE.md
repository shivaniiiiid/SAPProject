## üì± ANDROID INSTALLATION (Google Chrome or Edge)

### Step 1: Open the App in Browser
1. Open **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** app on phone
2. Type the AgriSmart URL in address bar and press Enter

### Step 2: Install the App
1. Look at the **bottom of screen** - you'll see a popup saying **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

4. The app will download (usually takes 10-30 seconds)

### Step 3: Open the App
1. After installation completes, **TAP "Open"** in the popup, OR
2. Go to your phone's **Home Screen**
3. Find the **AgriSmart icon** (green with wheat üåæ)
4. **TAP the icon** to open the app

## üîß TROUBLESHOOTING

### App Won't Install
- **Android**: Make sure you're using Chrome or Edge (not Safari)
- **iPhone**: Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome)
- Try again or check internet connection

### App is Slow
- Wait 10-30 seconds for first load
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Restart your phone

### Not Getting Updates
- Restart the app (close and reopen)
- The app auto-updates every minute when online

### Offline Not Working
- App needs internet at least once to download data
- After first use, it saves data for offline access

### Can't See the "Install" Button
- **Android**: The button appears after app fully loads (5-10 seconds)
- **iPhone**: Try refreshing the page and try again
- Try a different browser if available
